Language Patterns in Sentiment

Sentiment Analysis explained: A sentiment score for a word is based on polarity - where the underlying emotion conveyed is negative, positive or neutral. Polarity can be measured using a number of different natural language parsing and machine learning methods; perhaps most commonly (atleast that I've seen) via Naive Bayes classifier. In our case, the idea is that reading an individual post generally leaves you somewere on smiley to frowney face spectrum - a confession is the sum of its emotional language, and a sentences' net sentiment is the sum of its weighted word polarities. A typical mainstream application of sentiment analysis would be political opinion mining, of which there's boatloads of papers for an inquisitive pair of spectacles to peruse.
